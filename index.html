Web Design Projects

Cozmeena
www.Cozmeena.com

Cozmeena is a lifestyle brand of kindness, care and love. The Cozmeena website uses the Wordpress TwentyThirteen theme with custom post types for recipe and resource listings as well as style customizations.

Cozmeena.com also features an online store using the WooCommmerce plugin. The AddThis and MailChimp plugins provide social media and email marketing support.

HMF Express
www.HMFExpress.com

HMF Express is a manufacturer of quick-ship hollow metal doors. The HMF Express site uses the WordPress CMS platform and uses the RT-18 Theme with color and other customizations. The big challenge on the HMF Express site is providing the right information about the hundreds of products and customization options. This required significant re-coding of the "product" post type and the breadcrumb navigation.

Navigation uses the Ubermenu and ShiftNave plugins.

Church of the Nativity
www.NativityChurchNJ.org

The Nativity site was built using the Google Sites platform. Google Sites has an easy to use WYSIWYG interface and allows page-specific permissions. This means that the webmaster can retain control over the site while giving minimally-trained volunteers access to update their individual section of the website.

Google Sites is also free -- which helps the church spend more of it's money on the primary mission of community outreach and support.
